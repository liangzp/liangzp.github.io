Biography

Xiaoteng Ma is pursuing a Ph.D. in the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University. As a member of CFINS, he is supervised by Prof. Qianchuan Zhao and Prof. Li Xia (currently with Business School, Sun Yat-Sen University). He received his Bachelor of Engineering degree from Department of Automation, Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2017. His research interests include Markov decision process, reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems and game theory.
